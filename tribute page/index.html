The most importants dates of the Einstein's life

  • 1879 - Birth in Ulm (Germany)
  • 1900 - Diploma from the Polytechnic School of Zurich (Switzerland)
  • 1902 - Recruited by the Swiss Federal Patent Office (Switzerland)
  • 1905 - PhD from the University of Zurich; publication of four major scientific papers, including that of the formula E = mc2
  • 1913 - Professor at the Kaiser Wilhelm Institute, Berlin
  • 1916 - Publication of the Theory of General Relativity 1921 Nobel Prize in Physics
  • 1933 - Victim of Nazi anti-Semitism, he leaves Germany for the United States; teacher in Princeton (New Jersey)
  • 1955 - Death in Princeton
